Avtech Scientific joins OpenHPC initiative to integrate ASL into the HPC framework.

Dec 21, 2015

Avtech Scientific has joined OpenHPC, a collaborative effort to provide a new, open source stack supporting the world’s most sophisticated high-performance computing (HPC) environments. The initiative, which was formed by the Linux Foundation, includes more than 30 national laboratories, universities, research centers, and software vendors. These founding members have agreed to work together to create a stable environment for testing and validating HPC software. Avtech Scientific will integrate its open source accelerated multiphysics toolkit - Advanced Simulation Library into the framework.
